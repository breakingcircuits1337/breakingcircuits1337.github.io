About Me

I am a dedicated security researcher with first hand knowledge on how to overcome an APT style attack. Specializing in network security, system administration, and hardware expertise, I have developed a comprehensive skill set that spans operating systems, virtualization technologies, network configurations, and cybersecurity practices.

With a passion for both hardware and software aspects of security, I maintain a personal enterprise-grade lab environment for testing, research, and continuous skill development. This hands-on approach allows for practical application of theoretical security concepts and ensures that security implementations are thoroughly tested before deployment.

My expertise extends to custom firmware development for network devices, penetration testing methodologies, and AI security applications. As technology continues to evolve, I remain committed to staying at the forefront of security research, consistently expanding knowledge in emerging threat vectors and defense strategies.
